Submitting Assignment 5

Assignment 5 should be submitted using Marmoset, as follows:

  1. Download your A5.ipynb file from the CS hub to your computer.
  2. Change the name of your file from A5.ipynb to A5-xxxxxxxx.ipynb, where xxxxxxxx is your UW student ID number.
  3. Log in to Marmoset at https://marmoset.student.cs.uwaterloo.ca/ using your WatIAM password, and upload your re-named notebook file. Use the submit link for Assignment 5 to upload. You can see your previous submissions using the view link. You may submit multiple times - we will mark your latest (pre-deadline) submission.
